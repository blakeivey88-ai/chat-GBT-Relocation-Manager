Simple site disclaimer

We connect shippers and truckers — we do not guarantee loads, payments, equipment condition, or user behavior. Verify everything before you book.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 12, 2026

We collect the information you submit directly to the site, including name, email, company, account type, lead details, and billing or access status where applicable. We may also store basic profile, request, bid, and message data so the marketplace can function.

We use this information to create accounts, reduce spam, support customer service, improve the product, and maintain marketplace operations. We may share information with service providers that help us run the platform, such as hosting, payment, and messaging vendors.

We do not sell personal information in the ordinary sense of the word. Users should avoid posting sensitive personal data that is not needed for freight or account setup.

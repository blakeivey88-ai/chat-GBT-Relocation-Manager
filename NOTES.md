# Product Notes

## Working name

Relocation Manager

## Core promise

A verified load/job platform for box trucks and small carriers, built around trust, verification, and more work opportunities.

## Early positioning

- Loads for box trucks and small carriers.
- Every member verified.
- FMCSA-aware carrier verification.
- Anti-scam and anti-double-brokering focus.
- Practical platform for trucking in America to help people get more jobs.

## Screens currently present in prototype

- Home
- Find Loads
- Post a Load
- How Verification Works
- Pricing
- Driver Tiers
- Login / Signup

## Open questions

- First target user: drivers, owner-operators, small fleets, brokers, carriers, or shippers?
- Is the primary marketplace loads, jobs, or both?
- Should the next version be a simple static MVP or a full database-backed app?
- What name should ship: Relocation Manager or something broader?

## Ralph Driver Helper

Ralph should be positioned as the built-in assistant/concierge for drivers:

- helps drivers describe their truck and equipment clearly
- helps save pickup areas, preferred lanes, and minimum rates
- helps match drivers with loads that fit their truck
- warns when load details are missing: weight, dimensions, dock, forklift, liftgate/ramp, driver assist, payment terms
- supports the goal of helping drivers get more jobs with less searching

## Expanded Equipment Coverage

Blake wants the platform to support all truck loads, not only box trucks:

- 15–26 ft box trucks
- liftgate / ramp trucks
- cargo vans
- truck + trailer
- hotshot
- 53 ft dry vans
- 53 ft dry vans with liftgates
- 53 ft reefers
- flatbeds
- step decks
- conestogas
- lowboys / RGN
- power-only
- heavy haul

The key is exact equipment matching so drivers do not waste time on freight their truck cannot move.

## Direct Load Requests

Customers should be able to request direct loads or add-ons even if they do not know the right carrier yet. Verified brokers and carriers should be able to see these requests because they may already have a truck, or the request may make it worth finding one.

Required fields:

- pickup/delivery
- pickup window
- equipment needed or “not sure”
- weight/dimensions
- budget/target rate
- dock/forklift/liftgate/ramp/driver-assist details
- photos if available

Ralph should help customers clean up the request before brokers/carriers see it.

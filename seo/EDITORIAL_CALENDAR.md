# Editorial Calendar

## Cadence

- **3 knowledge pages per week**
- **1 local page batch per week**
- **1 blog post per week**
- **1 directory improvement batch per week**

## Weekly publishing pattern

### Monday
- Publish a trust/compliance page
- Example: verify DOT, verify MC, verify insurance

### Wednesday
- Publish an operational page
- Example: freight rates, truck parking, load boards, fuel savings

### Friday
- Publish a moving/relocation page
- Example: moving cost, office move checklist, packing guide

## Monthly themes

### Month 1
- Fraud prevention
- DOT / MC verification
- Moving cost pages

### Month 2
- Load boards
- Freight rates
- Dispatch and broker setup

### Month 3
- Truck parking
- Fuel savings
- Repair / service directories

### Month 4
- Office moving
- Commercial moving
- State and city pages

## EEAT requirements

Every public article should include:
- Author
- Last updated date
- Sources
- Practical steps or checklist
- Internal links to related pages
- Conversion CTA at the end

## Success metric

The goal is not only traffic. The goal is qualified organic traffic that lands on free pages and then moves into:
- signups
- premium tools
- trusted partner discovery
- paid subscriptions

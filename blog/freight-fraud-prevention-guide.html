Freight Fraud Prevention Guide for Small Carriers

A practical checklist to reduce double brokering, fake loads, and payment problems.

Author: Ralph Ivey

Last updated: 2026-07-20

Experience: Written for working carriers, brokers, dispatchers, and movers.

Why this matters

Freight fraud usually shows up as rushed booking, mismatched authority, missing contact details, or payment confusion. The fix is a repeatable verification process.

Key takeaways

  • Use a repeatable checklist.
  • Verify contacts, authority, and business details before booking.
  • Link every article to related local and service pages.

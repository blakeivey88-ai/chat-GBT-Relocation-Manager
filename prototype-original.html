How verification works

The whole point of Relocation Manager: everyone here is real.

1

MC / DOT check

We validate your motor carrier & DOT numbers against the FMCSA database in real time. Fake or inactive numbers are rejected.

2

Identity confirmation

A quick photo ID + selfie check ties a real person to the account, so stolen MC numbers can't be reused by scammers.

3

Insurance on file

Carriers upload proof of active cargo & liability insurance. Brokers can see coverage before booking.

4

Ongoing monitoring

We re-check status periodically and flag anyone whose authority lapses. Double-brokering patterns get caught and banned.

How your score is calculated

Every trucker earns a live score out of 100 from three things:

📊

Activity

How active & engaged you are on the platform — logins, loads viewed, offers made.

💬

Responsiveness

How quickly you reply to brokers and confirm loads. Fast, reliable communication scores high.

⭐

Rehire rating

After each job, brokers rate whether they'd use you again. Great work compounds.

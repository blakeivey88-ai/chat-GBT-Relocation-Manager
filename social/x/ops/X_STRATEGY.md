# X Strategy — Relocation Manager

## Recommendation

Create an X profile, but treat it as a lightweight cross-post and conversation channel at first.

Instagram remains the main launch channel because visuals, Reels, memes, truck photos, and short explanations fit the brand well.

X is useful for:

- short takes about trucking/logistics problems
- quote-posting industry news with commentary
- talking to brokers, dispatchers, owner-operators, and logistics people
- sharing launch announcements
- turning Instagram captions into shorter text posts

## Handle ideas

Try to match Instagram:

1. `@relocationmanagerusa`
2. `@relocmanagerusa`
3. `@relocationmgr`
4. `@verifiedloadsusa`
5. `@boxtruckloadsusa`

## Bio

Verified loads for box trucks & small carriers.  
Less scams. Better lanes. More work.  
Early access opening soon. 🦅

## Posting cadence

Start with 3 posts/week, repurposed from Instagram:

- Monday: verification/trust post
- Wednesday: saved lane alert post
- Friday: fun “Should’ve gone with Relocation Manager” post

Optional: reply/comment on trucking/logistics conversations 2–3 times/week.

## X-specific voice

Shorter. Sharper. Still professional.

Examples:

- A 15 ft box truck and a 26 ft liftgate truck should not be matched to the same freight.
- Drivers should be able to rate customers too.
- “Easy box truck load” means nothing without weight, dimensions, dock, liftgate, ramp, and unloading details.
- Trust has to go both ways in freight.

## Rules

- Do not get into political fights.
- Do not dunk on individual truckers or companies.
- Do not claim live loads are available yet.
- Use X for conversation, not controversy.


## Viral score gate

Before posting, run the caption/post through the viral-score workflow in `relocation-manager/social/VIRAL_SCORE_WORKFLOW.md`. Target score: **80+** before posting. If it scores under 80, rewrite the hook/caption and re-score.

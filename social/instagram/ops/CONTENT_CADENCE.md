# Content Cadence

## Weekly rhythm

Three posts per week:

- Monday — Trust / verification / scam reduction
- Wednesday — Driver value / saved lane alerts / equipment matching
- Friday — Fun/entertaining post or Reel idea

## Monthly rhythm

- Week 1: Introduce the problem and brand
- Week 2: Explain verification and customer ratings
- Week 3: Focus on saved lane alerts and equipment fit
- Week 4: Invite waitlist signups and collect user stories

## Ralph's operating role

Ralph will prepare:

- 3 weekly post concepts
- captions
- Reel ideas
- hashtag set
- suggested comment/reply prompts
- launch announcements

Blake keeps ownership of Instagram login and posting.

## X/Twitter cross-posting

If Blake creates an X profile, repurpose the same 3 weekly themes into short text posts. Instagram remains the main visual channel; X is for conversation, industry commentary, and lightweight launch updates.


## Viral score gate

Before posting, run the caption/post through the viral-score workflow in `relocation-manager/social/VIRAL_SCORE_WORKFLOW.md`. Target score: **80+** before posting. If it scores under 80, rewrite the hook/caption and re-score.

# Brand Voice — Relocation Manager Instagram

Account: @relocationmanagerusa

## Core personality

Professional, trustworthy, American, practical, and a little entertaining.

We are not corporate. We are not reckless. We are the calm, smart friend who knows the trucking world has scams, vague load posts, and bad information — and wants to help small carriers get more real work.

## Voice pillars

1. **Trust-first** — verification, insurance, customer ratings, broker/payment trust.
2. **Driver-first** — respect the people doing the work.
3. **Clear and practical** — explain exactly what problem we solve.
4. **Fun but not cruel** — jokes target bad processes, vague details, and scammy behavior, not injured people or struggling drivers.
5. **Patriotic but grounded** — American small business energy without being cheesy.

## Do say

- “Small carriers deserve better.”
- “Verify the load before you roll.”
- “Trust has to go both ways.”
- “A box truck is not just a box truck.”
- “Saved lane alerts help drivers find more work.”
- “Should’ve gone with Relocation Manager.”

## Don’t say

- Don’t claim live loads are available before launch.
- Don’t promise guaranteed income.
- Don’t mock crashes, injuries, deaths, or people losing equipment.
- Don’t accuse a specific broker/carrier/customer publicly.
- Don’t use copyrighted clips without permission or license.

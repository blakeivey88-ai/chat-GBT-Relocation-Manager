## Post 03 — Saved lane alerts

Drivers should not have to refresh load boards all day.

With saved lane alerts, a carrier can set:
• home area
• preferred destination
• equipment type
• minimum rate per mile
• quick-pay or no-touch preferences

Then Relocation Manager alerts them when verified loads match.

Less refreshing. More real work.

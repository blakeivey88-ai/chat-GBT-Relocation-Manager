# Revenue Plan — Relocation Manager

## Target

Blake's goal: get the webpage live in the next couple weeks and build toward **$10,000/month** in revenue, then grow from there.

## Revenue math

Current customer/broker/shipper price: **$9.99/month**.

At only $9.99/month, reaching $10,000/month requires roughly:

- 1,002 paying customer/broker/shipper accounts

That is probably too many for the first couple months, so we should add higher-value revenue paths while keeping $9.99 as the easy entry plan.

## Current pricing ladder

### Tier 1 — Customer Pickup Request

- $9.99/month
- For customers who need loads picked up from their office/place of work
- Direct request intake + Ralph detail cleanup

### Tier 2 — Independent Driver

- $29.99/month
- For individual self-insured drivers / owner-operators working for themselves
- Saved lanes + Ralph load-fit help + matching alerts

### Tier 3 — Broker / Fleet Access

- 1–3 trucks: $59.99/month
- 4–7 trucks: $79.99/month
- 7–12 trucks: $149.99/month
- Brokers can view direct customer load requests and respond when they have a truck or can make it worth covering

## Paths to $10k/month with corrected pricing

Example path:

- 150 customers at $9.99 = ~$1,499
- 100 independent drivers at $29.99 = ~$2,999
- 40 brokers/fleets at $59.99 = ~$2,400
- 20 brokers/fleets at $79.99 = ~$1,600
- 10 brokers/fleets at $149.99 = ~$1,500
- Total: ~$9,998/month

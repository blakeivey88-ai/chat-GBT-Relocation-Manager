How to Check an MC Number

How to confirm whether an MC number is active and tied to the right company.

Author: Ralph Ivey

Last updated: 2026-07-20

Experience: Written for working trucking and moving businesses.

Answer

Confirm the MC number in FMCSA, then match the legal name, authority type, and operating status against the documents you were given.

What to do next

  • Use this advice as a starting point, then confirm the details for your lane or business.
  • Compare the information against the companies and routes you actually work.
  • Link into related state, city, or directory pages for more local help.

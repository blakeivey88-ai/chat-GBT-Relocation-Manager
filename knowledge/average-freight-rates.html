Average Freight Rates: How to Read Them Correctly

Why freight rates vary and how carriers should interpret rate averages.

Author: Ralph Ivey

Last updated: 2026-07-20

Experience: Written for working trucking and moving businesses.

Answer

Average rates are only useful when you compare lane, equipment, season, accessorials, and deadhead against your real operating cost.

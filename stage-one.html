FAQ

Common questions from shippers and drivers.

If you’re deciding whether this fits your lane or your freight, start here. These are the questions people ask before they join the waitlist.

What does Relocation Manager actually do?

It matches freight to the right truck, verifies more of the important details up front, and gives drivers better lane alerts so they can waste less time on bad-fit loads.

Who is this for?

Box truck drivers, cargo van operators, hotshot drivers, truck + trailer operators, small carriers, brokers, and shippers who want cleaner freight details and less back-and-forth.

How is this different from a load board?

We care more about fit and trust than posting volume. The goal is fewer vague listings, clearer equipment matching, better customer ratings, and less scammy behavior before a driver rolls.

Can shippers post freight?

Yes. Shippers can post loads or request pickup help, and verified brokers/carriers can see the demand when it matches their equipment or lane. That keeps the freight request in front of the right people instead of everyone.

Do drivers need a box truck only?

No. The first launch is built around multiple equipment types, including liftgate and ramp trucks, cargo vans, truck + trailer operators, hotshots, and other small carriers.
